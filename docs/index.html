Candle

Candle is a haptic compass empowering visually impaired users through intuitive navigation. Harnessing advanced haptic feedback, it delivers real-time direction for confident exploration. Discover a new world of independence with Candle's groundbreaking approach to accessible travel.

Privacy Policy

This privacy policy is to inform users regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information for anyone deciding to use our Service.

Candle does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app is designed to work entirely on your device, ensuring your privacy and security.

The app does not require you to provide any personally identifiable information. We do not collect any personal data, and no information leaves your device.

Service Providers

Security

As we do not collect personal information, your use of Candle is secure. There is no transmission or storage of personal data.
